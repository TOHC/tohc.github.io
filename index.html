ABOUT US

The Toronto Hacker Club is a student-run organization aiming to promote computer science education in high schools throughout the Greater Toronto Area.

We host hackathons and workshops for high school students and have been awarded the "Building Community through Sciences and Technology Award" by the Toronto Youth Cabinet.
